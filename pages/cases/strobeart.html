Strobe - art

Online-service
Digital
Design service

Project
Overview

The service allows clients to order designer services for image processing. There is also additional functionality for automatically uploading processed images to Shopify, Amazon, and Instagram. When posting pictures, it is possible to automatically create a description for them using AI.

go to the website
  • Online service, Design service Tech
  • Website
  • 1month and 2 weeks

Project Goals

  • Create User-friendly Visuals /
  • Streamline Product Posting /
  • AI Title Generation /

An important goal was to create a visually appealing part that would be understandable to users. Facilitate the posting of new products to the online store. Using AI to generate accurate titles for images.

Solutions

  • User-friendly Design /
  • Advanced Functionality Development /
  • Precision AI Querying /

Our team of designers created a beautiful and attractive design that helped the end user easily navigate and utilize all the website's features. Developers used cutting-edge web technologies to create functionality that allows users to easily manage changes and upload images and other design items. It was important to create accurate queries to the AI to generate logically correct names for images, so our team spent a lot of time fine-tuning the AI settings.

Technologies Used

Challenges

The obstacle was in customizing the AI specifically for our system because without adjustments, the AI could provide inaccurate results. However, our team was able to fine-tune it effectively.

The client had specific requirements regarding the project's visual appearance, so based on examples and ideas, we were able to align closely with the client's vision.

The system was supposed to have a seamless process of processing and uploading various types of images, and thanks to individual research in this direction, we were able to make this process uninterrupted.

Project phases

The Roadmap

The client opted for a Time and Material task approach, so we processed ideas from the client and formulated tasks for the team based on them, for further implementation and testing.

Phase 1

Research & Design

During the research phase, we were able to conduct a brief business analysis and comparative market statistics of competitors within the allocated time frame for effective data gathering. Based on this research, we skillfully implemented client ideas into business tasks in Figma design.

Phase 2

Development & Testing

We were able to engage specialists of various types and directions in the development process, who worked on tasks related to front-end development, backend work, and AI implementation settings. Thanks to coordinated teamwork, we were able to implement all assigned tasks successfully. We tested the system and successfully carried out the process of bug fixing.

Phase 3

Maintenance

After successful development, we remained in contact with the client and continued our collaboration, receiving new tasks for implementing fresh ideas from the client. Additionally, we proposed a series of ideas to the client from our side, which they agreed to implement in the project to improve the system.

Conclusion

Summary

We are sincerely grateful to the client for the opportunity to work on such an interesting project as Strobe-art. Here we were able to successfully implement the client's ideas into a beautiful system that now helps users. We also deepened our knowledge regarding the implementation of AI in such systems. It is also important to note that the client's holistic view of the project gave us the right direction in implementing the online service for designers.
